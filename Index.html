SatEx Systems About Info

Founded in 2025, SatEx Systems is a British aerospace company headquartered in Bristol, England, United Kingdom. We specialize in delivering end-to-end satellite solutions, covering the complete mission lifecycle — from initial mission design and engineering, through manufacturing, integration, and rigorous testing, to operational management and end-of-life planning. Our expertise spans across commercial, scientific, and governmental space missions, ensuring innovative, reliable, and cost-effective solutions that meet the highest industry standards. At SatEx Systems, we are committed to advancing the capabilities of satellite technology, supporting our clients’ ambitions in Earth observation, communications, navigation, and beyond.

Solutions

ADCS Development and Manufacture

Power your mission with our advanced Attitude Determination and Control Systems. From star trackers and reaction wheels to sun sensors and magnetorquers, our components deliver unmatched accuracy, low power use, and proven reliability in space. Keep your satellite exactly where it needs to be — every time.

ADCS Testing Equipments

Our ADCS Testbed is a cutting-edge platform that helps satellite teams design, test, and refine their attitude control systems before launch. It simulates real space conditions, reducing risks and ensuring your satellite performs flawlessly in orbit.

Satellite Educational Kit

Our Satellite Education Kit brings the world of satellites to your classroom or lab. Perfect for students and professionals, it offers hands-on learning in satellite systems, communications, and control, bridging theory with real-world practice. Learn, experiment, and innovate—your journey to space starts here.

Software solutions
